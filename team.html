Popess coming from the land where the dead coexist with the living. She is Leyenda’s heart, guides the team as the brightest light in the sky. Always radiant, knows how to light the darkest paths so that the group doesn’t lose their way.

Cosmic traveler of a thousand worlds. As a game librarian possesses the hidden knowledge to alter the rules of reality, thereby creating unique universes. He has traveled through countless worlds through roleplaying games.

PEDRO LEONETTI

Assembled cyborg with the ability to visualize all of reality’s variants. Thanks to his storage capacity he discovered how to distill what Leyenda’s book should look like. He developed his specialty in virtual simulators before dedicating himself to roleplaying games.

LEONARDO LUJÁN

One of a kind and outstanding alchemist. He has discovered the transmutation techniques able to materialize ideas. The world is his canvas and his strokes create monsters, heroes and civilizations. His hand has brought Leyenda’s worlds to life.
