COMPANION

FOR YOUR LEYENDA GAMES

To play Leyenda you first have to choose your role in the game: Director (in charge of Framing Scenes and arbitrate on the rules), or Player (in charge of deciding what one of the Protagonists of the game will do), or Chorus (in charge of judging, expand and comment on what happens on the session). In this Companion we will condense step by step what your role does in every moment of the game.
